## 🏷️ Application Overview

DMD Gold Prosperity is an **end-to-end B2B digital platform** built exclusively for the Indian jewellery trade. It connects **Wholesalers / Manufacturers** (sellers) with **Retailers** (buyers) on a single, unified platform — replacing manual processes like phone calls, WhatsApp catalogues, handwritten bills, and Excel tracking with a modern, GST-compliant, and fully digital experience.

> **एक platform — पूरा jewellery business digital!**

### 2. 🏷️ Advanced Product Listing & Stepper-Based Creation

- **Step-by-step product creation** with a guided stepper flow:
  1. **Select Category** (Super Category → Category → Sub Category)
  2. **Add Product Details** (name, SKU, weight, carat, purity, metal type, colour)
  3. **Add Gems/Diamond Details** (for studded jewellery — full 4C parameters)
  4. **Upload Images** (multiple product photos)
  5. **Review & Submit** (comprehensive summary before going live)

- **Category Hierarchy**: Super Category → Category → Sub Category (e.g., Jewellery → Necklace → Choker)
- **Auto-generated SKU numbers** for each product
- **Visibility Controls**: Set products as Visible or Hidden
- **Sales Channel**: Mark as Online, Offline, or Both
- **Stock Status**: In Stock / Out of Stock tracking
- **Gender Tags**: Male, Female, Kids, Unisex

> **USP:** "Jaise aap dukaan mein maal rakhtay ho — waise hi digital mein rakho. Step-by-step, bilkul simple!"

### 4. 🧾 Comprehensive Invoice System — "GST-compliant, government-ready"

| Invoice Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Jewellery Invoice** | Standard purchase invoice for jewellery (CGST + SGST / IGST auto-calculation) |
| **Metal Invoice** | For gold, silver, platinum, titanium metal transactions |
| **Diamond/Gems Invoice** | Specialized invoice for diamonds (1.5% GST) and gems (0.25% GST) — separate tax slabs |
| **Outward Challan** | When sending goods to karigar or another party without sale |
| **Inward Challan** | When receiving goods back from karigar |
| **Labour Invoice** | For labour/making charges paid to karigars |
| **URD Invoice** | Unregistered Dealer invoice for purchases from non-GST parties |
| **E-Invoice** | GST E-Invoice generation and management for compliance |

- **Invoice List** with search, filter, and export to Excel
- **GST Auto-Calculation** for CGST, SGST, IGST, UTGST, and TCS
- **Union Territory Support** (Andaman, Chandigarh, Delhi, J&K, Ladakh, etc.)
- **Customer/Company Search** within invoices
- **Karigar (Artisan) Address** linking in invoices

> **USP:** "Pehle invoice banane mein 30 minute lagte the. Ab 3 minute mein GST-compliant invoice ready — E-Invoice bhi!"
